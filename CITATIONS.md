## Facial Landmark Detection

**LBF facemark model** (fitted to extract the 68 facial landmarks):

Ren, S., Cao, X., Wei, Y., & Sun, J. (2014). Face alignment at 3000 FPS via
regressing local binary features. *CVPR*, pp. 1685–1692.
https://doi.org/10.1109/CVPR.2014.218

**iBUG 68-point landmark annotation standard** (numbering convention for landmark
indices used throughout the feature engineering code):

Sagonas, C., Tzimiropoulos, G., Zafeiriou, S., & Pantic, M. (2013). 300 faces
in-the-wild challenge: The first facial landmark localisation challenge.
*IEEE ICCV Workshops*, pp. 397–403.
https://ibug.doc.ic.ac.uk/resources/facial-point-annotations/

## Eyewear Frame Recommendation Rules

The face-shape-to-frame pairings in `src/visionaid/recommendation.py` follow
consensus guidelines from optician and styling literature:

- Ellison, J. (2012). *The Eyeglass Style Guide*. Optical Women's Association.
  General principle: frame geometry should contrast with face geometry.

- Bhatt, K. (2014). Face shape and eyewear selection. *Review of Optometry*,
  151(11). https://reviewofoptometry.com (accessed April 2026).

- Eckman, M. (2018). *Fashion: The Industry and Its Careers* (3rd ed.).
  Fairchild Books. pp. 212–215.
